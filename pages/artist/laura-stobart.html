Laura Stobart

I grew up in Zimbabwe. The highlights of my childhood were our family camping trips, often to friends' farms back in the day when farming and wildlife co-existed quite peacefully. My bush-loving parents instilled in me a deep love for the animals, birds, and plants of the Zimbabwean bushveld. Africa owns my soul! When my artist husband taught me how to wield a paintbrush, painting African birds, especially raptors, became my passion.

Enjoy the paintings!
