Coreart Gallery Welcome

"Providing a market and a platform for artwork by Zimbabwean artist."

Coreart is the vehicle that provides and links artwork by local artist with consumers. The ambition for Coreart is to open strategic galleries over the coming years that promote and expose the most talented artists from Zimbabwe.

Coreart Founders

Coreart was founded by Sean Webster, a proud Zimbabwean with an ambition to help others. Having been an artist himself, exhibiting in Uk, USA, New Zealand and around Southern Africa.

Sean has had some exposure to the Art Industry previously as an artist but now no longer paints. He has chosen to focus on helping other artists get thier work out. By creating Coreart, and starting with a gallery in Marondera.
